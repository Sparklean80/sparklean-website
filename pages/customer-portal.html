Install on your phone

Add Sparklean to your Home Screen

There is no Download button — Apple requires you to add the app from Safari. After you sign in, follow the steps below.

iPhone (Safari)

  1. Tap Open Your App above — use Safari, not Gmail’s browser
  2. If the link opened inside Gmail: tap ··· → Open in Safari
  3. Sign in with your email — we send a secure magic link
  4. At the bottom of Safari, tap Share (square with arrow)
  5. Scroll → tap Add to Home Screen, then Add

Android (Chrome)

  1. Tap Open Your App above, or open your welcome email link
  2. Sign in with your email on file
  3. Tap the menu (⋮)
  4. Tap Install app or Add to Home screen

Open from your home screen icon for full-screen app mode — no browser bar, no App Store.

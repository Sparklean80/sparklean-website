What Fort Myers Businesses Need From a Commercial Cleaner

Fort Myers commercial spaces range from waterfront professional suites to high-traffic retail plazas. A real janitorial partner handles:

  • Reception and waiting-area dusting, vacuuming, and glass
  • Restroom sanitization and supply restocking
  • Break rooms, kitchens, and staff common areas
  • Hard-floor care for tile, LVT, and polished concrete
  • After-hours access with bonded, insured crews

Why “DIY” Often Costs More Than a Cleaning Service

Many Bonita Springs homeowners try to handle everything themselves — then realize:

  • Time is the hidden expense. Deep cleaning kitchens, bathrooms, and floors eats entire Saturdays.
  • Consistency slips first. Dust returns quickly; baseboards and ceiling fans get skipped when you are tired.
  • Wear and tear shows up in grout, glass, and finishes when soil sits too long.

A recurring home cleaning plan with Sparklean keeps the baseline high so you only touch up between visits, not start from zero every week.

High-Intent Signs You Should Call Sparklean Today

  • You need the home “show-ready” for guests, seasonal arrival, or a rental turnover.
  • Allergies or pets mean you want floors and dust handled on a schedule.
  • You are juggling work, travel, or family and cleaning keeps falling to the bottom of the list.
  • You want recurring peace of mind — predictable visits and predictable results.

What a Professional Residential Cleaning Visit Typically Includes

Every home is different, but a strong standard home cleaning usually covers:

  • Kitchen counters, appliance fronts, and sink area
  • Bathrooms: toilets, tubs/showers, vanities, mirrors
  • Dusting of reachable surfaces
  • Vacuuming and hard-floor mopping
  • Trash emptied (where agreed)

Deep cleaning or move-in/move-out service adds detail work — baseboards, interior windows, cabinet fronts, and heavier buildup. For construction dust, see our post-construction program before switching to maintenance.

Maid Service vs. Full Cleaning Service

Maid service usually means recurring maintenance on a weekly or bi-weekly schedule. A full cleaning service adds deep cleans, move-in/move-out detail, post-construction cleanup, and white glove care — all under one supervised Sparklean team.

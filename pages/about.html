Our story

Two journeys,
one standard

Sparklean Cleaning was built from two very different professional journeys that shared one belief: people deserve a higher standard of care, professionalism, and trust inside their homes.

Founded by Tony Giuliano and Roxana “Roxy” Tellez, Sparklean was created to redefine what residential cleaning should feel like — not rushed, transactional, or inconsistent, but intentional, refined, and deeply service-oriented.

✦
Leadership & care
The operational foundation

National-scale leadership,
applied at home

Before founding Sparklean, Tony Giuliano earned his Master’s Degree from the University of Maryland and built his career in operations, leadership, and client experience at a national level. As a Regional Vice President for CORT Trade Show & Event Furnishings, he oversaw large-scale operations, logistics, client relationships, and service execution across demanding luxury and corporate environments.

That experience shaped the foundation of Sparklean.

What homeowners were missing

The problem was rarely
just “cleaning.”

Tony saw that the cleaning industry often lacked what luxury homeowners truly value most:

  • Professionalism
  • Communication
  • Accountability
  • Presentation
  • Consistency

The problem was rarely just “cleaning.” It was the experience surrounding it.

Clients were forced to manage unreliable scheduling, inconsistent quality, poor communication, rushed visits, and companies that treated homes like transactions instead of personal spaces.

Sparklean was built to change that.

A different kind of discipline

Care shaped by
service under pressure

At the same time, Roxy brought a completely different level of care and discipline to the company. Before coming to the United States, she worked as a doctor in Cuba — a background that shaped her attention to detail, composure under pressure, and deep respect for serving people.

That mindset became part of Sparklean’s culture.

To Roxy, caring for someone’s home is personal. It requires trust, responsibility, precision, and pride in the details others overlook.

Together

Operational leadership meets
genuine human care

Together, Tony and Roxy combined operational leadership with genuine human care to build a company centered around one core idea:

Luxury is not just appearance.

Luxury is consistency.

Luxury is feeling cared for.

Luxury is knowing every detail was handled intentionally.

Today

Southwest Florida homes,
the same intentional standard

Where we serve
Today, Sparklean Cleaning serves homeowners across Naples, Bonita Springs, Estero, Fort Myers, and surrounding Southwest Florida communities with a focus on elevated residential cleaning, recurring maintenance, post-construction detailing, and curated add-on services designed for high-standard homes.
Built around people
Beyond the services themselves, Sparklean is built around people. Many members of the Sparklean team come from hardworking backgrounds and understand the value of opportunity, discipline, and pride in their work. Every team member is trained not only in cleaning standards, but in professionalism, discretion, presentation, and respect for the homes they enter.

From luxury kitchens and waterfront estates to family homes preparing for guests, every visit is approached with the same mindset: show up professionally, communicate clearly, care deeply, and leave the space better than you found it.
